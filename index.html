• Country Club & Golf Course

    Discover the stunning championship golf course at The Villages, nestled in the picturesque Evergreen district's rolling foothills in southeastern San Jose.
    Designed by renowned architect Robert Muir Graves, this par-72 gem is one of Northern California's premier courses, offering 18 holes with four tee
    sets to suit all skill levels. Additionally, The Villages features a charming nine-hole, par-3 course, perfect for both beginners and experienced golfers
    looking to enhance their skills.
